Located in Cannawigra just out of Bordertown, Warrensville Poll Hereford Stud is a family run passion as much as it is business.

Producing the highest quality stud cattle, with a caring approach to the way they are raised, feeding primarily off grass, the way nature intended it to be.

We have a wide range of products and services on offer:

  • Stud bulls, cows and heifas
  • Meat
  • Hay bales.
