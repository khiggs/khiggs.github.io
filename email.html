YOUR INVITATION

It seems like yesterday when we were planning our first Warrensville Production Sale for March 1974. I remember fondly our sale of Warrensville Fairvern for $11,000 to Charlie Lee, Leelands Poll Hereford stud and Scottie Johnson Lochnager Poll Hereford Stud, both in New Zealand.

Our Annual Bull sale is held in March each year, with a field day held in February to meet our stud bulls on offer.

View our sale catalogue from our March sale.

Delivery: While every care will be taken, no responsibility can be accepted whilst awaiting delivery. Interstate Purchases: The vendors agree to hold any stock for interstate buyers until any necessary tests have been completed. Expenses incurred payable by purchaser. Should any cattle react, the sale will be cancelled. Health: All Warrensville cattle are officially government accredited as TB and Brucellosis Free and MN2 accredited.
